THE INSTABOOTH

We love photos

The InstaBooth was created to streamline the party experiance. Drawing inspiration from classic photo booths colliding with modern social media this is the next step in photography. The functionality is simple; hit a button and take a photo. Users can choose either to take a burst of 5 photos, or a premium InstaPhoto which will be imediately uploaded to the booth's instagram account. The photo burst option takes five photos in sequence and saves them to post to the Instagram account later, so dont't worry if they don't appear immediately. This may take a day or two as there is a post quota of 25 images per day

INSTRUCTIONS

Everything you need to know about the InstaBooth


Follow the on-screen instructions to take a five photo burst, or an InstaPhoto, or quit the program. Please be aware that every photo taken on the InstaBooth will be uploaded to a public instagram account. If you have concerns for your privacy, please do not use the software

.

Photo Burst

take a burst of 5 photos

Press 'b' to initialise a photo burst. Five photos will be taken and uploaded when the booth is next in standby mode.

InstaPhoto

upload one photo directally to instagram

Press 'i' to intitialise a single InstaPhoto. The photo will be immediately uploaded to Instagram, unless the daily quota of 25 has been reached, in which case it will be uploaded at a later date.

Standby Mode

pause and process

The photo booth can only uplod 25 photos over a rolling 24 hour period. To ensure all 5 PhotoBursts are uploaded to the Instagram page, the booth should be put into standby mode by pressing 's' at the end of the session. This will enable all photos to be uploaded as soon as the 25 photo a day quota allows.
